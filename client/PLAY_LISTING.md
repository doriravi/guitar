## Full description (max 4000 chars)
```
FretFit helps guitar players practice smarter — right from your phone, fully
offline, no account required.

Built for real hands: FretFit scores how hard each chord shape is to reach on a
1–10 scale, so you can find voicings that actually fit your fingers instead of
fighting stretches that don't.

WHAT'S INSIDE
• Tuner — accurate real-time pitch detection to get every string in tune.
• Chord library — see the shape of any chord, with a difficulty score for the
  reach it demands.
• Scale practice — pick a scale and play it; FretFit listens through your mic and
  scores your accuracy, speed, and how well you know the fretboard.
• Progressions & songs — practice chord changes, the part that's actually hard.
• Live listen — play into the mic and see what note you're hitting.

HONEST BY DESIGN
FretFit uses your microphone to hear the notes you play. It can tell you the pitch
you're playing — it does not watch your hands or track your fingers, and it's
upfront about what it can and can't know.

PRIVACY-FIRST
Guest mode works completely offline. Camera and microphone are used on your device
for the tuner and practice features — audio and images are processed on the phone
and are not uploaded in guest mode.

No ads. No account needed to start. Just pick up your guitar and play.
```
